ABOUT US


Rail & Sail Logistics (Pty) Ltd is a Supply Chain Solutions Company.  Our main objective is to provide the best
possible solutions and services for our clients, taking away the additional
burden of planning, following up and seeing through of shipments.  With many successful years within the freight
industry, our team of hardworking, driven and passionate staff, will ensure
that our clients receive the best service, always.

Rail & Sail Logistics (Pty) Ltd specialises in :

Sea Freight

Export & Import (Normal & Hazardous Cargo)

Road Freight

Export & Import (Normal & Hazardous Cargo)

Air Freight

Export & Import (Normal & Hazardous Cargo)

International Courier

Export & Imports (Documentation & Smaller Parcels)

Hazardous Compliance is very important in our industry and to ensure that our clients meet the required criteria when it comes to hazardous shipments, we have reliable local suppliers for all the necessary Labels, UN Packaging, Trem Cards / Paperwork for transportation purposes, which means that our clients cargo is moved from point A to point B, safely, securely and accurately.

Having world wide partners, we are able to offer forwarding, clearing and delivery to just about anywhere in the world.

Being passionate about what we do, shows our clients that we are passionate about what they do.
